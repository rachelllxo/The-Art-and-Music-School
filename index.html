About Us

Exam Centre for London College of Music (LCME), Rock School London(RSL), Trinity College London. Specialised in Classical Guitar Education, Keyboard, Piano & Violin.

Our Courses

  • Guitar

    • Learn to play the guitar from experienced instructor. Our guitar course covers everything from basic chords to advanced techniques. Receive personalized feedback and support to help you progress at your own pace.

  • Keyboard

    • Our keyboard course is designed to teach you everything from reading music to playing complex pieces, suitable for all skill levels. You will develop strong foundational skills while enjoying creative expression through music.

  • Violin

    • Discover the joy of playing the violin with our comprehensive course, which includes both classical and contemporary styles. Whether you're a beginner or looking to refine your skills, our course adapts to your unique journey.

  • Art

    • Drawings and Paintings: Our classes range from beginners to Advance levels to enhance creativity.
