About Us

Exam Centre for London College of Music (LCME), Rock School London (RSL), Trinity College London. Specialized in Classical Guitar Education, Keyboard, Piano & Violin.

Our Courses

  • Guitar

    • Learn to play the guitar from our experienced instructor, starting with the basics and progressing to advanced techniques. Classes include chords, strumming patterns, fingerstyle, music theory, and performance skills. Whether you want to play for fun or perform on stage, our program will guide you every step of the way.

  • Keyboard

    • Learn everything from reading sheet music to performing complex pieces, with lessons tailored for all levels. Our structured approach includes scales, chord progressions, sight-reading, improvisation, and accompaniment skills, helping you become a confident and versatile keyboard player.

  • Violin

    • Master famous classical pieces and explore a variety of musical styles with guidance from expert instructors. Lessons focus on posture, bowing techniques, sight-reading, and musical expression, ensuring that students develop both technical precision and emotional depth in their performances.

  • Art

    • Enhance your creative flow through various forms of art and painting, exploring styles from realism to abstract. Our sessions cover sketching, color theory, composition, and mixed media techniques, allowing you to develop your unique artistic voice in a supportive and inspiring environment.
